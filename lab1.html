The Solar System

Introduction to Our Solar System

The Solar System is a vast and fascinating place that includes the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and more. It is the foundation of our cosmic neighborhood.

Planets of the Solar System

The Solar System consists of eight planets, each with unique characteristics. They are divided into terrestrial and gas giants.

General Facts

  • The Sun makes up 99.86% of the Solar System’s mass.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.
  • Venus is the hottest planet, even hotter than Mercury.
